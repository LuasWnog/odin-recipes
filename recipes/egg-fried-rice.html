Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Water (1 cup)
  • Soy Sauce (2 tbsp)
  • Salt (1/2 tsp)
  • Day-old rice (1 cup)
  • Vegetable Oil (1 tsp)
  • Onion, finely chopoed (1/2 onion)
  • Egg, lightly beaten (2 eggs)
  • Ground black pepper (To taste)

Steps

  1. Bring water, soy sauce, and salt to a boil in a saucepan. Stir in day-old rice and remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a wok over medium heat. Saute onions in hot oil for 2-3 minutes. Pour in beaten egg and fry for 2 minutes
  3. Add cooked rice to egg mixture. Season with pepper

Enjoy!
