Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices (Parsley, basil leaves, salt, black pepper)
  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Cheeses (Mozarella, ricotta, parmesan)
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Layer lasagna:
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Pasta
    3. Ricotta
    4. Mozarella
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan
    7. Repeat
    8. Parmesan
  2. Cook lasagna (Oven, 375 Fahrenheit, 50 mins)
